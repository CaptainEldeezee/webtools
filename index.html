Avatar to Be

The character/avatar I've decided to use for this class is one that I've already conceived before, that of an old seafaring captain. "Captain Eldeezee" is his name, and is in fact just my initials sounded out into a word. "L-D-Z" becomes "El-dee-zee". The visual look of him is just the Simpsons sea captain character. As seen: His character comes through the text most obviously. Lots of stereotypical seafaring lingo. “Arr, me mateys” and so on. Other than that, he’s a bit of a one-dimensional character, mostly just a funny persona that I put on. So he has no established backstory. His “ship” is in fact the website, that is how he refers to it.
